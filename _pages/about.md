## Current Work

My current work lies at the intersection of <strong>autonomous driving</strong>, <strong>decision and control systems</strong>, and <strong>industrial R&amp;D</strong>.

- At <strong>Stellantis</strong>, I contribute to the development of <strong>control algorithms for autonomous driving and ADAS</strong>, with applications to vehicle dynamics and motion control.
- My doctoral research focuses on <strong>multi-controller architectures for automated driving in heterogeneous and critical scenarios</strong>.
- More broadly, I am interested in the development of reliable autonomous systems that combine <strong>modeling, simulation, control, estimation, and experimental validation</strong>.
